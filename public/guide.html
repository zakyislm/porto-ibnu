Portfolio User Guide

A quick manual on how to manage and update your portfolio content.

1 Accessing the Dashboard

To start editing your portfolio, you need to log in to the Admin Dashboard.

  • Add /login to the end of your website URL (e.g., yourwebsite.com/login).
  • Click the Continue with Google button.
  • Sign in using your authorized Google account.

2 Profile

The Profile tab allows you to update your main identity and website meta details.

  • Personal Info: Update your name, job title, short bio, and location.
  • Images & Files: Upload a new profile picture and your latest CV document.
  • About Section: Edit the detailed text that appears in your "Who I Am" section.
  • Web Title: Update the text that appears on the browser tab.

3 Projects & Experience

Keep your portfolio updated with your latest work and history.

  • Projects: Add new projects with titles, descriptions, and cover images. You can use the up/down arrows to reorder how they appear on the live site.
  • Experience: Add your educational background, organization history, or work experience. You can arrange them chronologically using the reorder buttons.

4 Skills & Values

Highlight what you are good at and what you stand for.

  • Values: Add core principles that define your work ethic (e.g., "Empathy First").
  • Skills: Add specific skills as tags. Use the category dropdown to group them (e.g., Soft Skills vs. Hard Skills).

5 Social Links & Access Settings

The Settings tab helps you manage connectivity and security.

  • Social Links: Add links to your LinkedIn, Instagram, etc. The system will automatically detect the platform based on the URL and display the correct icon in your footer.
  • Email Contact: If you add an "Email" link, the Contact Form on the homepage will automatically route messages to that address.
  • Whitelisted Admins: Want to give someone else access to edit your portfolio? Add their Google Email here. (Note: Only authorized emails can log in).
Important Note: Always remember to click the Save button at the bottom of the screen after making changes. If you try to navigate away without saving, your changes will be lost!
